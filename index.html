About me


Research Driven & Organised

I'm a highly organised and independent thinker with an eye for visual appeal. I enjoy exploring a variety of hobbies, which keeps me curious and creative, and I thrive when I can structure my work and projects in a way thats both efficient and aesthetically pleasing. I'm always looking for ways to bring creativity and clarity together in everything I do.

I enjoy working across both digital and visual design, especially the process of moving from rough sketches to more polished outcomes. Seeing an idea evolve into something tangible is one of the most rewarding parts of studying design. I’m keen to keep developing my skills, building a strong body of work and gaining experience that challenges me to think more creatively and confidently.

How I work

My Process

01 — Discover

Research & Understanding

Research, competitive analysis, heuristic evaluation. Understanding the problem space deeply before proposing any solution.

02 — Define

Synthesis & Framing

Affinity mapping, journey maps, personas, and problem statements. Narrowing to the design opportunity with clarity and intent.

03 — Design

Concept to Prototype

Lo-fi sketching through to polished prototypes using a variety of sofwares.

04 — Deliver

Refine & Deliver

Testing and refining. Making sure the final outcome hasn't strayed away from the original problem. Taking on feedback and applying it.
